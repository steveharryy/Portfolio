Overview

SwasthGuru is a comprehensive premium health dashboard designed to bridge the gap between patients and medical professionals. Built during a competitive hackathon, it features real-time appointments, secure data synchronization, and an AI-powered first-aid chatbot ("SwasthSewak") integrated directly into the core platform architecture.

Problem

Patients often struggle with fragmented health records, lack of immediate first-aid medical guidance, and complicated booking interfaces. The challenge was to build a secure, real-time, HIPAA-compliant feeling platform that could rapidly match patients with available doctors while providing AI backup for sudden medical queries.

Solution & Features

We architected a unified health-tech ecosystem utilizing WebRTC for seamless video consultations and dynamic environment-aware backend endpoints. Key features include an AI health assistant, synchronized patient/doctor dashboards, dynamic medicine tracking with geolocation, and a robust Next.js authentication flow protected by Clerk.

Tech Stack
