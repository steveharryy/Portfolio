Overview

Vichaar Setu is a digital bridge designed to connect young innovators with potential investors. Built to foster startup culture, the platform allows students to showcase their visionary projects while providing investors with a curated stream of high-potential opportunities to mentor and fund.

Key Features

The platform features a multi-tiered idea submission system, a dedicated investor dashboard for project discovery, and integrated evaluation tools to track project growth and funding milestones.
